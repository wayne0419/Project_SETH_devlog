Q: Why video extration takes so long, how long does it take?

A: Most of the time spent are on downloading video and data from Twitch. A 4-hrs-long stream video recording will take roughly 15 mins to finish extraction.

Q: Why the site said that my Twitch video URL is not found?

A: This may be caused by the video is too old(more than 1 month ago and get kicked out from Twitch's database) or too new(yeah...try it several hours later).
